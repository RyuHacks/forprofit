Our Story

Qalam Consulting began as a pro-bono initiative to assist struggling businesses within our community in the face of economic uncertainty, rapid technological development, and heightened competition. It was understanding the different challenges businesses faced as well as the prohibitive cost of consulting services that led us to create Qalam Consulting. Our experience helping clients across various industries is what led us to develop Qalam Consulting into a for-profit consulting firm that helps businesses solve their greatest challenges.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help businesses overcome their toughest challenges by providing thoroughly researched and results-driven solutions that are practical, impactful, and accessible to organizations of all sizes.
