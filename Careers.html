What it’s like working at Qalam

Working at Qalam
We believe in delivering assistance to businesses that genuinely improves their operation. We have always believed since our start as a pro-bono initiative that consulting should be made affordable and accessible to benefit all businesses facing difficult challenges. This dream is what motivates us to deliver the consulting services our clients love.
Why Work for Qalam?
  • Remote Work Environment
    Embrace the freedom to work from anywhere. We provide the tools and support you need to be productive and connected, no matter your location.
  • Competitive Compensation
    We value your expertise and contribution, offering a comprehensive compensation package that aligns with industry standards and rewards performance.
  • Learning and Tuition Reimbursement
    Invest in your growth. We support your educational pursuits and continuous learning with a generous tuition reimbursement program.
  • Flexible Work Schedule
    We trust you to manage your time. Our focus is on results, not rigid hours, allowing you to create a schedule that fosters work-life balance.
  • Company-Wide Wellness Days
    Your well-being is a priority. We schedule paid company-wide wellness days to ensure everyone has time to unplug, recharge, and focus on health.
  • Generous PTO Policy
    Take the time you need to rest and explore. Our flexible paid time off policy empowers you to enjoy life outside of work.
  • Professional Development
    Advance your career with access to mentorship, specialized training programs, and clear pathways for growth within the company.
